Problem :

As you scale, your customers are constantly giving you Feedbacks, Ideas and Bugs to make the product perfect. So, you need a platform that collects all the feedback, sorts them with priority by voting, discusses them both publicly and privately via team collaboration, and more. GetFeedy is trying to fill that gap.

Challenge :

Despite their large number, there are very few feedback management tools that combine what founders need for making a better product with the help of customers and their team with better UI/UX.

Journey :

Designed version V1 (August, 2019)

Generally, dribble designs are fancy, but I learned every fancy design doesn't always work better. I did all the hard work to make it look good, but often, my clients had problems with the user experience. By doing so, I am inspired to make a better version V2 by overcoming mistakes.


As part of this version, I used Adobe XD and learned about it

Designed with Adobe XD

Designed version V2 (May, 2021)

The design of the current version involved lots of research, from data arranging to sketching to get the information on one screen with as few as possible clicks while maintaining an easy user experience. The design is a result of my research and the collaborative effort of my team members.


In June 2021, We launched a Product Hunt campaign and won the #1st Product Of The Day Badge.

Every step I took taught me some new fundamentals for improved UI/UX. Still learning.
