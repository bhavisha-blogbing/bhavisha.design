Overview

GetMentions will help you to monitor your web and social mentions on a single dashboard with email alerts.

Started on

December . 2018

Team

Jenish Bimbha

(Front-End Developer)

Scope of work

Competitive Research

Information architecture

User Research

Visual hierarchy

What I did :

Designed a SaaS (B2B/B2C) tool for businesses and individuals who are interested in monitoring their brand mentions for more leads and quick communication. Main goal to achieve that on a single screen with clean UI/UX. Developer helped develop pixel-perfect design. Designed landing page and graphics as well.


Would you like to join? Dashboard: https://app.getmentions.io/login

Currently in Beta (working on improving Quality of the Mentions).

Problem :

A marketer needs to spy on its potential leads daily. It is a tedious process to check all social media and the web to find quality mentions of their brand.

Challenge :

While numerous, only a very small number are designed for a single-screen experience with rich visuals.

Journey :

Designed version V1 (December, 2018)

The version I created had no prior experience with design or basic coding. Trying my hand at Adobe Illustrator, I created this version. 

Designed with Adobe Illustrator

Designed version V2 (January, 2021)

In order to try to make perfect cards within the screen, I conducted competitor research and combined it with a visual hierarchy of data. A single-screen will make it easily accessible to everyone.

Designed with Figma

Learning :

  • Design requires innovation with the copy. (The entire world revolves around copy and innovation) 
  • How to create a visual hierarchy of data that shows data priority
  • Creating this version taught me more about Figma.
