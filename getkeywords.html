Problem :

It is a basic SEO tool, which everyone needs, but it simplifies data visualizations in a way that is very few in the market.

Challenge :

With appropriate charts, it's difficult to render text data in visualization format. My current focus is on the visualization of text-based data; I am currently conducting research on this and thinking about how I should approach it.
